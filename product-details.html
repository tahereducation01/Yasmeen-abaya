Cold Hand Wash

Gently hand wash in cold water (max 30°C) with mild detergent. Avoid wringing or twisting the fabric.

Air Dry Only

Lay flat or hang to air dry in shade. Do not tumble dry or expose to direct sunlight for extended periods.

Low Heat Iron

Iron on low heat setting with a pressing cloth between the iron and the fabric. Steam ironing is recommended.

No Bleach

Never use bleach or harsh chemicals. They will damage the silk fibres and fade the hand-stitched detailing.

Dry Clean Preferred

For best results, professional dry cleaning is recommended, especially after formal events.

Storage

Store in the provided dust bag in a cool, dry place. Avoid folding — hang on a padded hanger to maintain shape.
